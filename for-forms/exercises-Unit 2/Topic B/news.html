Company news

[Last updated 12/22/12]

ISO certification

Outlander Spices is now ISO 9000 certified, reflecting our commitment to providing only the finest quality products.

Bulk packages

We now offer the majority of our products in money-saving bulk packages. These are usually 32-ounce packages sealed in vacuum bags to ensure freshness. Our bulk products are guaranteed fresh because we prepare and grind our spices at the point of sale.

New hot sauces and salsas

Try our new range of hot sauces and salsas from around the world! We offer a large selection to suit every taste. If you're a heat enthusiast, we have some of the hottest sauces on the planet for you to explore.
